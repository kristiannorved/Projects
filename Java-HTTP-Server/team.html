MEET OUR TEAM

Daniel Wilson

Dan is the lead pastor at Indooroopilly Evangelical. Dan joined us in 2015 with his wife Rachel and daughters Jess, Sophie, and Isabel. Dan has a deep passion for the gospel, and desires for everyone in South Brisbane and beyond to hear about the saving death and resurrection of Jesus Christ. In his spare time, Daniel enjoys spending time with his family and fishing.

Ezekiel Jensen

Ezekiel is our youth pastor here at Indooroopilly Evangelical, and has been since 2019. As such, Ezekiel is responsible for coordinating all youth and children's events, such as Friday night youth, Sunday school for our morning service, and our holiday kids club events. Ezekiel is married to Emma and together they have two boys: John and Matthew.

Noel Ivanov

Noel is a student minister at Indooroopilly Evangelical. Noel is in his third year of theological training at Queensland Theological College, and expects to graduate at the end of 2024. In his spare time, Noel enjoys bike riding and playing video games.
