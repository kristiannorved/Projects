JOIN US!

We've got ministries for everyone! All are welcome!

Morning Church
Sundays 9am

Our morning service is for everyone - young and old. We are blessed to have people of all ages join together each Sunday to worship our Lord and saviour Jesus Christ.

Night Church
Sundays 5pm

Our night service is primarily for university students and early graduate workers. We also have several high school students and some older persons as well! All are welcome!

Friday Night Youth
Fridays 6pm

Our Friday night youth program is for all youth in grades 7 to 12. We play some games, eat some food, and have the Bible read and explained. It's lots of fun!
